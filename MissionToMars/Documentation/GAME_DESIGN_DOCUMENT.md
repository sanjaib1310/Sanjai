# Mission to Mars - Game Design Document

## Executive Summary

**Mission to Mars** is a professional AAA-quality 3D educational game combining immersive space exploration with comprehensive STEM learning. Players assume the role of a young astronaut progressing through 100 unique missions across 10 worlds, from Earth's Space Center to Mars' underground research facilities.

## 2. Story & Setting

### Timeline
Year 2045 - A future where space exploration is widely accessible and emphasized in education

### Premise
The player is selected as a promising young astronaut to join the prestigious International Mars Academy. Their journey begins with foundational training on Earth and culminates in establishing humanity's first student-led research station on Mars.

### Narrative Arc
1. **Earth Academy** - Foundation training and basic mission control
2. **Earth Orbit** - ISS operations and satellite management
3. **Lunar Operations** - Moon base establishment
4. **Deep Space Navigation** - Asteroid field challenges
5. **Planetary Approach** - Mars orbit insertion
6. **Mars Exploration** - Surface missions and scientific discovery
7. **Advanced Challenges** - Valles Marineris and Olympus Mons
8. **Settlement** - Establishing the research colony
9. **Mastery** - Advanced terraforming and deep exploration
10. **Legacy** - Becoming the First Student Mars Commander

## 3. Gameplay Mechanics

### Movement & Controls
- **Walking/Running**: Standard locomotion with sprint boost
- **Jumping**: Overcome small obstacles
- **Jetpack**: Low-gravity propulsion (limited fuel)
- **Spacecraft Piloting**: Navigate between worlds
- **Rover Operation**: Surface exploration on Mars

### Core Gameplay Loop
1. Receive mission briefing
2. Navigate to objective location
3. Solve educational challenges (Math/Astronomy)
4. Complete scientific tasks (Repair, Collect, Build)
5. Return to mission control
6. Earn rewards and unlock progression

### Challenge Types
- **Mathematical Puzzles**: Orbital mechanics, distance calculations, physics equations
- **Astronomy Quizzes**: Solar system knowledge, planetary facts
- **Navigation Challenges**: Route planning, fuel management
- **Equipment Repairs**: System diagnostics and fixes
- **Environmental Hazards**: Radiation zones, dust storms, debris avoidance
- **Time-Pressure Missions**: Complete objectives within time limits

## 6. Progression System

### Level System
- **100 Total Levels** (10 Worlds × 10 Missions)
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Gradually increases through worlds
- **No Repetition**: Every puzzle/challenge is unique
- **Prerequisite System**: Complete previous world to unlock next

### Experience & Currency
- **XP (Experience Points)**: Earned per mission completion
- **Coins**: In-game currency for upgrades
- **Stars**: Quality metric (1-3 stars per mission)
- **Achievements**: Special unlocks and milestones

### Unlock System
- **New Spacecraft**: Unlock upgrades and new vehicles
- **Space Suits**: Specialized equipment for different environments
- **Planets/Worlds**: Progress through solar system
- **Vehicles**: Mars rovers, jetpacks, specialized tools
- **Research Labs**: Unlock new scientific facilities
- **Scientific Tools**: Advanced measurement and analysis equipment

---

## 7. Reward System

### Mission Completion Rewards
- **Base XP**: 100-300 per mission
- **Base Coins**: 50-150 per mission
- **Base Stars**: 1 (up to 3 for perfect completion)

### Bonus Multipliers
- **Difficulty Multiplier**: Easy (1x), Medium (2x), Hard (3x)
- **Perfect Completion Bonus**: 1.5x XP/Coins, 3 stars
- **Speed Run Bonus**: 1.25x XP/Coins, +1 star
- **Challenge Modifier**: +25-50% for extreme conditions

### Achievement System
- **First Steps**: Complete World 1
- **Space Traveler**: Complete World 5
- **Mars Pioneer**: Complete World 7 (Mars Surface)
- **Red Planet Master**: Complete all 100 missions
- **Math Genius**: Solve 50 mathematical puzzles
- **Astronomy Expert**: Answer 50 astronomy questions correctly
- **Speed Runner**: Complete mission in half the time
- **Perfect Score**: Complete mission with perfect accuracy
- **Equipment Collector**: Unlock all available equipment
- **Student Commander**: Final achievement - complete game
